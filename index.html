About Me

Hi! I'm Amir, an independent game and software developer who finds joy in blending technology with creativity. From concept to release, I love bringing playful ideas to life, especially when it means designing games for animals like cats and dogs.

My projects are often inspired by nature, behavior, and emotional connections. I strive to make my apps simple, beautiful, and user-friendly. I'm currently focused on interactive pet games, mobile applications, and user-centered design.
